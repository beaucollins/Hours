Sext

Written by Pr. Jami Fecher

“Your Kingdom come…”

On this hour your beloved Son died on the cross, winning for you and for me your kingdom. Rule with your love in me and in your Church. Thank you for your steadfast faithfulness. Stir me to trust you more fully. Whatever I feel this day, whatever circumstances I find myself in, I am in your hands. I know you work all things together for my good and the good of your whole creation. Give me courage to act on this assurance of your goodness and power in Jesus Christ my Lord.
