Morning Prayer

Upon Waking

Written by Pr. Jami Fecher

“Our Father in Heaven…”

O Lord, open my lips and my mouth shall declare your praise: You are my heavenly Father and I am truly your child. You want me. I know this by your promise and the mercy you have shown me my whole life long. Help me to pray and think only of you, pouring out all I am to you, counting on you alone, and offering you my thanks and praise in Jesus’ name.

“…hallowed be your name.”

You are holy and so is your name, worthy of all praise and honor. Thank you for making me your child and for allowing me to bear your name. Help me to live like I bear your name, to live in harmony with your word, and to encourage others to live in harmony with it. Thank yovu for all the lessons this day that direct me to you, through Jesus Christ.

(This prayer can be followed by Bible readings, confession and prayers for others)
