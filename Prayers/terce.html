Terce

Written by Pr. Jami Fecher

“…hallowed be your name.”

You are holy and so is your name, worthy of all praise and honor. Thank you for making me your child and for allowing me to bear your name. Help me to live like I bear your name, to live in harmony with your word, and to encourage others to live in harmony with it. Thank you for all the lessons this day that direct me to you, through Jesus Christ.
